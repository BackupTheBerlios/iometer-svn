Introduction

As the Iometer User's Guide says, Iometer is an I/O subsystem measurement and characterization tool for single and clustered systems. It was developed by the Intel Corporation and its availability was announced at the Intel Developers Forum (IDF) on February 17, 1998.

Meanwhile Intel has discontinued to work on Iometer and it was given to the Open Source Development Lab (OSDL). A project was registered at SourceForge.net and a initial drop was provided. After a time of silence, the project has been relaunched in February 2003 and is now driven by a group of individuals in co-operation with the OSDL.

The tool is distributed under the terms of the Intel Open Source License.
